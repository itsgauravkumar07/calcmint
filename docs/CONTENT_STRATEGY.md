# CalcMint Content Strategy

## Overview

CalcMint follows a **Topical Authority SEO Strategy** rather than publishing random finance articles.

Every calculator has its own content cluster consisting of guides, comparisons, FAQs, and supporting educational articles.

The objective is to become the most comprehensive finance resource in each topic.

# Content Writing Rules

* Write for beginners first.
* Keep language simple.
* Use real-world examples.
* Avoid unnecessary financial jargon.
* Answer the user's question quickly.
* Include a calculator within the article whenever relevant.
